Expense Insights & Key Takeaways
Payroll Dominates
83%

Payroll accounts for $295K of $354K in 2025 operating expenses — the single largest cost driver by far. Any meaningful expense reduction starts here.

Payroll is Highly Volatile
$4.8K → $47.6K

2025 monthly payroll ranged from $4,800 (Jan) to $47,561 (Apr) — a std dev of $13K. This suggests commission-based or irregular pay cycles that are difficult to forecast.

Efficiency Leap in 2026
75% → 31%

Expense-to-income ratio dropped from 75% (Jan–Apr 2025) to 31% (Jan–Apr 2026). Op expenses fell 24% while income nearly doubled — a major profitability inflection.

Buro – Xactus Growing Fast
+93% YoY

Average monthly Buro cost grew from $2,481 (2025) to $3,533 (2026 Jan–Apr). This is volume-driven — more credit inquiries means higher loan pipeline activity, a positive signal.

Software Cost Spike in Feb 2026
$5,377

Software jumped to $5,377 in Feb 2026 vs. an avg of $1,581/month in 2025 (+240%). This one-time spike warrants review — likely a new tool or annual renewal front-loaded into February.

Other & Software = Well Controlled
8.2% combined

Software (5.4%) and Other (2.8%) together represent only $29K of $354K in 2025. These costs are stable and predictable — the team runs a lean SaaS stack.

Expense Category Descriptions
Payroll

Includes all salary payments to W-2 employees (Payroll USD) and fees to external contractors and outsourcing partners based in the U.S. and Colombia (Payroll Col — Premier Spaces, Marco Materrazi, Juan Carlos Arce, Mara Celedon, Esystems Cybersecurity).

Software

Core sales and productivity platforms used daily by the team: RingCentral (phone/VoIP), Phoneburner (dialer), Jungo (CRM), Calendly (scheduling), Arive (loan origination), DocuSign (e-signatures), and Monday.com (project management).

Buro – Xactus

Monthly subscription to Xactus, the credit bureau and data analytics platform used to pull tri-merge credit reports for borrowers. Cost varies by volume of credit inquiries processed each month.

Other

Miscellaneous operating software and minor subscriptions not classified above: QuickBooks (accounting), Adobe (design), LinkedIn (recruiting/marketing), Loansifter (pricing engine), Open AI, Google Workspace, GoDaddy, Sinch Mailgun, and similar tools.

Not Operating Expenses

Expenses not directly tied to the core mortgage brokerage operation. Includes: Meals & Entertainment (client lunches, team snacks, travel meals), Travel (airfare, hotels, transportation), Rent & Phone (office space, mobile plans), Marketing (ads, business licenses, promotions), Administrative (office supplies, tools, legal fees, accounting, insurance, repairs), and Vehicle & Other (gas, parking, tolls, auto repairs, bank fees, interest, depreciation). Excluded from operating expense totals by default.
